About me

I am a constantly evolving Graphic Designer, dedicated to improving my skills and striving for excellence in my field. My journey as a Graphic Designer began in 2018 when I joined the team at Nova Concursos, a renowned company specializing in public exam preparation in Brazil. In this role, I created artwork for the company's Instagram posts and also managed the content strategy, planning, creating, and scheduling the posts. I was also involved in video editing and creating motion graphics for promotional materials, YouTube intros, and other social media assets.


In 2021, I had the opportunity to join Plural Cursos, a company focused on producing corporate training courses . Initially, I was responsible for conceptualizing and creating videos for training purposes. With dedication and quick learning, I developed skills in tools such as Articulate Storyline and Genially , which allowed me to grow within the company. I am currently a Senior Graphic Designer , involved in all aspects of course production, from visual identity conceptualization using Photoshop and Illustrator to course programming using tools like Articulate Storyline, Rise, and Genially .


Always eager to learn and stay updated, I pursued a degree in Analysis and Systems Development , which provided me with additional skills in web design, including HTML, CSS, and JavaScript . I am currently furthering my knowledge in UX Design through a course at DIO (Digital Innovation One).


My dream is to relocate to Canada with my family and apply my knowledge and skills in a new country. I have an advanced level of English, as evidenced by my EF Set test score of 74/100 (equivalent to C2 level in CEFR), and I am also starting to study French.


I am excited to take on new challenges and continue growing professionally in my journey as a Graphic Designer.
